Do Hard Things

(00:47 mins, "Do Hard Things" Transcript)

In many instances, we are asked to do hard things. We do them and become better for having done so. This semester you are going to be asked to do hard (or, at least, unfamiliar) things. Work hard, collaborate and find success.

Unit 1 Overview

This unit covers course and tooling foundations, getting to know one another, teaching presentation selection, and getting started with building a backend-based web application.

Team Meetings

Learning teams meet weekly, beginning in week 2. Attendance is required and part of the class grading. Plan a time when each member of the team can be in attendance.

Checkboxes and Questions

Click the checkbox when done to indicate that the item is complete. Below each section are areas to write any questions you have. This should help you remember questions that arise, and can be posted to the discussion board, asked in class or the team meeting.
