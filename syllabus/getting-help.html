Getting Help

There are multiple avenues of help, but keep in mind that you must have tried to solve the problem yourself prior to seeking help from others.

Your Peers - it is expected and required for you to work together in your learning teams to help one another by meeting face-to-face, calling one another, using virtual software (MS Teams, Google Meet, etc...).

Discussion Board - use the weekly discussion board to post and answer questions related to the topic of the week. You can also share insights and outside resources that can benefit others.

Teaching Assistant - contact the teaching assistant to get help and answer your questions.

Professor - Click the "professor" button on the Home page for information on how to schedule an appointment with the professor.

Open Lab there is a drop-in lab in the STC building. There you will find lab assistants who can help you during its hours of operation.
