Render.com Account

Introduction

From this point forward, we will maintain a more professional workflow in our couse. This means that we will develop and test locally and push our finished work to a remote production environment - Render.com. In order to get started, you mush have an account.

Create your Account

  1. Navigate in a browser tab to Render.com.
  2. Click the "Get Started" button in the top-right corner.
  3. Click the "GitHub" button. - By using the OAuth option (login method) and tying the Render.com and GitHub accounts together, it will make our workflow easier in the long run.
  4. You should be shown a screen asking you to authorize and login to your GitHub account. By positively answering the prompts and logging in to your GitHub account, the two accounts will be authorized to work together.
