MVC Interactions

Introduction

As you use the server.js file, the route and view you will notice that there are multiple components working together to determine what is being requested, where the resource is located, and finally how it will be built from a variety of smaller code segments and finally delivered back to the client browser. This process is commonly referred to as the Model - View - Controller design pattern. In this unit, not all of these are used, but they will be as we progress in the course.

You are being introduced to these concepts now, in the hope that you will work diligently to understand and comprehend the role each plays in the process of developing and delivering content from the backend to the frontend.
